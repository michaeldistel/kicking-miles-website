### Korean Bike Path Network

This adventure will primarily follow South Korea's renowned bike path system, designed for long-distance cycling but perfect for kick scooter travel:

**Nakdonggang Bike Path**

- Starting from Busan, following the Nakdong River
- South Korea's longest river bike path (~300km total)
- Connects coastal Busan to interior river valleys
- Well-maintained surface with rest stops and facilities

**Saejae Bike Path**

- Historic mountain pass route connecting regions
- Traditional trading route through scenic highlands
- Cultural significance as ancient pilgrimage path
- Challenging but rewarding terrain

**Namhangang Bike Path**

- Following the Nam Han River system
- Rural countryside and traditional villages
- Peaceful riverside scenery away from major highways
- Connection point between southern and central regions

**Hangang Bike Path**

- Final approach along the Han River into Seoul
- Urban bike path through Korea's capital region
- Modern infrastructure with city skyline views
- Triumphant finish in the heart of Seoul

These interconnected paths will provide safe, scenic routes while avoiding heavy traffic on major highways. The bike path network includes regular rest stations, cafes, and accommodation options designed for long-distance travelers.
